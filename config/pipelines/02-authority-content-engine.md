# Pipeline 2: Authority Content Engine

> A recurring pipeline that produces consistent thought leadership content optimized for both organic visibility and paid amplification.

## Trigger
- Weekly/bi-weekly content production cycle
- New content pillar identified by Agent 03
- Trending industry topic detected by Agent 12

## Frequency
Runs on a recurring schedule (typically weekly or bi-weekly per client).

## Pipeline Steps

### Step 1: Topic Identification & Research
**Agent:** 03 - Authority Content Strategist
**Action:** Identify high-opportunity topics from keyword research and competitive gaps
**Inputs:**
- Editorial calendar (existing)
- Client industry and content pillars
- AHREFS/Semrush keyword data
- Competitor content analysis
- Trending topics from Agent 12
**Outputs:**
- Content brief with target keyword, search intent, and angle
- Competitive content analysis (what exists, gaps to exploit)
- Recommended content format (article, case study, white paper)
- Internal linking targets
- Distribution plan (organic + paid amplification)
**Quality Gate:** Topic has validated search volume and clear differentiation angle
**Estimated Duration:** 1-2 hours

---

### Step 2: Content Production
**Agent:** 04 - Authority Copywriter
**Action:** Produce long-form authority content piece
**Inputs:**
- Content brief (from Step 1)
- Client brand guidelines and voice standards
- Subject matter reference materials
- Previous high-performing content examples (from Agent 18)
**Outputs:**
- Long-form article (2,000-5,000 words) with proper citations
- Meta title and description (SEO-optimized)
- Key takeaways / executive summary
- Pull quotes for social media use
- Author bio and E-E-A-T signals
**Quality Gate:** Passes readability, plagiarism, factual accuracy, and brand voice checks
**Estimated Duration:** 3-6 hours

---

### Step 3: SEO Optimization
**Agent:** 10 - SEO & Organic Growth Manager
**Action:** Optimize content for on-page SEO factors
**Inputs:**
- Draft content (from Step 2)
- Target keyword and related terms (from Step 1)
- Site's existing content and internal linking map
**Outputs:**
- SEO-optimized content with on-page improvements
- Internal linking recommendations (specific anchor text + URLs)
- Schema markup (Article, FAQ, HowTo as appropriate)
- Image alt text recommendations
- Content optimization score
**Quality Gate:** On-page SEO score above 85/100, no keyword stuffing
**Estimated Duration:** 1-2 hours

---

### Step 4: Social Content Derivation
**Agent:** 11 - Social Media Organic Manager
**Action:** Create platform-native social content derived from the authority piece
**Inputs:**
- Final optimized content (from Step 3)
- Pull quotes (from Step 2)
- Client social media profiles and posting schedule
**Outputs:**
- LinkedIn article or post series (2-3 posts)
- Twitter/X thread (5-10 tweets)
- Instagram carousel (8-10 slides with copy)
- Facebook post with engagement prompt
- Social media posting schedule
**Quality Gate:** Each post is platform-native, not just copy-pasted excerpts
**Parallel With:** Step 5 (can run simultaneously)
**Estimated Duration:** 2-3 hours

---

### Step 5: Paid Amplification Creative
**Agent:** 05 - Ad Creative Director
**Action:** Create paid advertising creatives to promote the authority content
**Inputs:**
- Final content piece (from Step 3)
- Key takeaways and pull quotes (from Step 2)
- Brand visual guidelines
- Target audience for content amplification
**Outputs:**
- Content promotion ad images (2-3 variants)
- Video teaser script (15s format)
- Ad copy variants for content promotion
- Audience targeting recommendations for content amplification
**Quality Gate:** Creatives drive curiosity without giving away all content value
**Parallel With:** Step 4 (can run simultaneously)
**Estimated Duration:** 2-3 hours

---

### Step 6: Performance Tracking & Learning
**Agent:** 16 - Client Reporting & Insights Compiler
**Action:** Track content performance and feed learnings back to the content strategy
**Inputs:**
- Published content URLs
- Social post engagement data
- Paid amplification performance data
- Organic ranking progress (from Agent 10)
**Outputs:**
- Content performance report (page views, time on page, conversions)
- Social engagement summary
- Paid amplification ROI analysis
- Learnings and recommendations for future content
- Update to Agent 18 knowledge base
**Quality Gate:** Report includes actionable next-steps, not just data
**Estimated Duration:** 1-2 hours (runs 7-14 days after publication)

---

## Recurring Cycle

After Step 6 completes:
1. Learnings feed back into Step 1 for the next content piece
2. Agent 18 updates the knowledge base with performance patterns
3. Agent 03 adjusts the editorial calendar based on results
4. High-performing topics get expanded into content series
5. Underperforming formats get deprioritized

## Total Estimated Duration Per Cycle
- **Content production:** 8-16 hours of agent work
- **Including human review:** 2-3 business days per content piece
- **Performance tracking:** Automated, runs on schedule post-publication
